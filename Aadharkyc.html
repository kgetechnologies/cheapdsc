What is Paperless DSC Issuance?

This facility is enabled for applying Digital Signature Certificates (DSC) in a paperless manner using "Aadhaar Paperless Offline eKYC". To use this facility, subscriber must be an Aadhaar holder with updated Mobile Number.

Get Digital Signature Certificate in few simple steps

Step 1 eKYC Enrolment

Open eKYC Account using your Aadhaar Paperless Offline eKYC

Step 2 Apply DSC

Select Digital Signature Certificate of your choice

Step 3 eKYC & eSign

Authenticate and perform eSign using eKYC Account credentials with OTP

Step 4 Video Recording

Record video while answering simple questions
Obtain your DSC in minutes* after processing by eMudhra representative.
Prerequisites
Please download your Aadhaar Paperless Offline eKYC from UIDAI website - Click Here.
Step 1 eKYC Account Enrolment - Upload the Aadhaar eKYC file, enter the Share Code, Mobile Number (registered with Aadhaar) and provide other necessary information asked for. Then verify your eKYC information and submit the same to complete the account creation process.
Step 2 Apply Certificate page - In eKYC enrolment success page, click on APPLY DSC button and choose the digital certificate of your choice and click on PROCEED.
Step 3 Verify & eSign page - Verify the Application information and provide the eKYC login credentials for eSigning the DSC application. Now your DSC application will get submitted and will show the option to Record Video, as this is mandatory for eKYC account activation.
Step 4 Video Recording - Once the Video Recording is completed, the application will be processed by eMudhra, and respective certificate download instruction email will be sent to your registered email address.
